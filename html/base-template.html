🤖 The Gist

When releasing a prototype product, like Hey Rebekah, it's hard to decide what tools to use.

Each new shiny object promises to make our lives easier.

We decided to adopt a tool crafted by experts who had a solid track record with newsletters. We hoped this shiny gem would illuminate our path to success.

But as we started using it, we discovered its sheen was, at times, a double-edged sword.

The tool's creators released updates so frequently that it disrupted our well-laid plans. The faults outshined the positives. We found ourselves blinded and unable to progress towards our grand vision.

Embracing the respected shiny object tools can still be a good practice for most.

But we must learn to sift through the sparkle to find the true diamonds on our journey to success.
